My Own Anchor Phrases
Write the phrases that bring you back when you drift.
Month of

Sit with these questions at the end of every month. They are the north star of the journal.

"Am I spending the majority of my time on work that serves my Compass Line?"
"What has changed about how I understand my purpose since last month?"
"What habit or system made the biggest difference this month?"
"Where have I been drifting — and what brought me back?"
"What is the one thing next month must accomplish to keep me moving in the right direction?"
"If the version of me from one year ago could see this month, what would they say?"

This journal is a tool in service of a direction that is already inside you.
Return to it not as a duty, but as a compass check.
